A Recent Entrance to Paradise, an image generated and named by DABUS in 2012. Courtesy Stephen Thaler.

AI artwork created by Steven Thaler failed to apply for copyright protection by the U.S. Copyright Office Review Board. (06/02/2022)

  • U.S. Copyright Office Review Board stated that they refuse to register a claim if it determines that a human being did not create the work. Human authorship is a prerequisite for copyright protection.
  • Steven Thaler claims he has ownership of the machine, and register the work as a work-for-hire because he owns the Creativity Machine, but was rejected again.
  • The Board did not address under which circumstances human involvement in machine-created works might meet the statutory requirements for copyright protection.

AI Creative Arts

AI creative art combines the features of both AI and digital art. It can be an autonomous artwork created entirely by AI algorithms or a collaborative work between humans and the AI system.

Artificial intelligence (AI)

  • Computer system
  • Imitate or perform more effectively than human intelligence, such as problem-solving and experience-based learning (Seshia et al., 2022).

Digital Art

  • Uses digital technologies to create artworks that can be displayed on digital media.
  • Computer software, hardware and specialised tools are used to create digital art.
